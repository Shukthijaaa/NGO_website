About Basti Ki Pathshala

Creating Equal Educational Opportunities for Every Child

📖
Our Story

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is a Government-registered NGO under the Indian Societies Act of 1860. We started with a mission to break the barriers of education in underserved communities. Our classrooms began in open spaces within slums, but the dreams have always been sky-high.

🎯
Our Mission

To provide quality education and life skills to children in marginalized communities. We strive to ensure every child is given the chance to learn, grow, and lead.

📚
What We Do

Through volunteer-led teaching programs, community-driven campaigns, and innovative tools, we create safe, inclusive learning environments. From literacy to leadership — we build it together.

🌟
Our Vision

A future where no child is left behind due to their background. We envision slum communities empowered through education and dignity.
